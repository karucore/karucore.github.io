KaruCore is a RISC-V processor core developed by Manse Processors -- SoC Hub and Information Security Laboratory, [**Tampere University, Finland**](https://www.tuni.fi/en/tau/computing-sciences).

This site is a place for notes, documentation, and updates on the project. It is
maintained by [Markku-Juhani O. Saarinen](https://mjos.fi).

## Contact

Found a bug, have a question, or want to get in touch? Open an issue on
[GitHub](https://github.com/karucore) or send an [email](mailto:mjos@iki.fi).
